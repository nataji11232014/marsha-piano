About Marsha

Marsha Emmons is an experienced piano teacher with a passion for helping students discover the joy of music. With over 40 years of teaching experience, Marsha tailors each lesson to fit the student's unique goals and interests.

Her teaching style is patient, encouraging, and adaptable to each student's learning style.

She welcomes students of all ages, from young children to adults.

Marsha believes that learning piano should be fun and rewarding.

Outside of teaching, Marsha enjoys composing her own music, and spending time with family and friends. She is dedicated to fostering a vibrant musical community.
